FAQ's

Quick answers to common questions. Click a question to expand.

01. WooCommerce or Shopify—how do we choose?

WooCommerce is great if you want WordPress flexibility; Shopify is excellent for managed hosting and built-in commerce tools. I’ll recommend a platform based on your catalog size, budget, and features.

02. Can you migrate products from another platform?

Yes—CSV imports from Squarespace, Wix, or Etsy are common. I’ll help map titles, SKUs, variants, prices, and images to the new store’s structure.

03. Do you handle payments and PCI compliance?

I configure trusted gateways (e.g., Stripe/PayPal/Square) that handle PCI compliance. Your store never stores card data; the gateway processes it securely.

04. How long does an e-commerce build take?

Most shops launch in 6–10 weeks once product content is ready. Timelines vary with catalog size, custom features, and integrations.
