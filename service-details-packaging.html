FAQ's

Quick answers to common questions. Click a question to expand.

01. What file types do I receive?

Print-ready PDFs (with bleeds/crops/CMYK) plus PNG/JPG for digital. Source files (AI/PSD/INDD as applicable) are included when the project completes.

02. Can you handle printing?

Yes—I'll prep files to your printer’s specs and can coordinate quotes and delivery if needed.

03. How fast is turnaround?

Single projects are typically 3–7 days once content is ready. Bundles are 1–2 weeks. Rush options are available for time-sensitive campaigns.

Package Design

I create packaging that tells your brand story and survives the realities of print and shipping. You’ll get smart structure, clear hierarchy, and accurate files—so what you approved on screen is what lands in your hands.

  • Structure & Dielines: Custom dielines or vendor templates with glue flaps, bleeds, and tolerances.
  • Material Guidance: Board weight, coatings, foils/emboss, windows, and sustainable options.
  • Brand & Typography: Clear hierarchy for flavor/sku, sub-brand, and legal copy.
  • Compliance Panels: UPC/GS1 barcodes, net wt/qty, ingredients, nutrition/allergens (cosmetics/food ready).
  • Print Production: CMYK/spot color builds, varnish/foil layers, overprint/trapping where required.
  • Mockups & 3D Proofs: Flat proofs plus photoreal mockups for stakeholder review.
More capabilities
  • Insert/Tray Design: Protective inners, sleeves, and fit tests.
  • Finish Libraries: Soft-touch, matte/gloss, spot UV, foil, emboss/deboss.
  • Series Systems: Multi-flavor/sku frameworks with easy colorway swaps.
  • Short-Run & Digital: Small-batch setups for pilots or limited editions.
  • Vendor Coordination: Quotes, spec sheets, preflight, and press checks.
  • Sustainability: FSC stocks, recycled content, soy inks, minimal waste layouts.
  • Retail Readiness: Planogram notes, hang tabs, and shelf callouts.
  • Regulatory Details: FDA/INCI panels, batch/date codes, recycling marks.
  • Shipping Reality: Edge crush, scuff mitigation, carton labels, and ship tests.
  • Localization: Multilingual panels and regulatory variations by market.

We start with goals, constraints, and the product’s realities—dimensions, materials, unit economics, and retail/ship conditions. I develop structure and hierarchy first, then design routes and finish options. Once a direction is chosen, I finalize the dieline, color builds, and all content panels with clear, legible typography.

Before production, I preflight every file (bleeds, traps, overprint, resolution), verify barcodes and compliance panels, and supply layered press-ready PDFs plus native files. You’ll receive a flat proof, 3D/mockup renders, and a vendor packet with specs and notes. I can coordinate quotes, proofs, and optional press checks to ensure the run prints exactly as approved.
