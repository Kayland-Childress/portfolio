01. Do you provide hosting?

I don’t resell hosting, but I’m happy to recommend providers and coordinate with your host. Care plans work with your existing host./p>

02. What counts as a “content edit”?

Website Care & Support

I keep your WordPress/Elementor site fast, secure, and up to date. Core/plugin updates, backups, security monitoring, and small content edits are handled for you—with reporting and quick help when you need it.

  • Safe Updates: WordPress, theme, and plugins updated with checks.
  • Backups: Automated, versioned backups with quick restore if needed.
  • Security: Monitoring, firewall/hardening, and spam protection.
  • Uptime & Performance: Alerts, caching/CDN guidance, and image tuning.
  • Content Edits: Small text/image swaps and minor layout tweaks.
  • Reporting: Monthly summary of updates, uptime, and key metrics.
More capabilities
  • Staging & Rollbacks: Test risky updates before going live.
  • License Management: Track premium plugin/theme renewals.
  • Form Deliverability: SMTP setup plus SPF/DKIM/DMARC checks.
  • SEO Housekeeping: Meta, redirects, and broken link scans.
  • Emergency Restore: Fast rollback using recent backups if an update causes issues.
  • Issue Triage: Diagnose plugin/theme conflicts and apply hotfixes.
  • Accessibility Touch-ups: Contrast, type sizing, and alt text.
  • Database & Media Cleanup: Optimize tables and media bloat.
  • Quarterly Tune-up: Speed audit and actionable recommendations.

Each month I schedule safe updates (core/plugins/themes), verify the site, and take fresh backups. I watch uptime/security alerts, handle small content edits, and send a concise report so you know what changed and why.

Routine maintenance covers updates, backups, monitoring, and minor edits. New features, redesigns, custom development, or platform migrations are quoted separately. I’m happy to advise on hosting, DNS, and licenses and can coordinate as needed.
