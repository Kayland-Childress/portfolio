FAQ's

Quick answers to common questions. Click a question to expand.

01. How many concepts and revisions are included?

Essentials includes 1–2 concepts and 2 revisions; Identity includes 2–3 concepts and 3 revisions. Additional rounds are available as add-ons.

02. Do I receive source files and ownership?

Yes—final vector masters (SVG/EPS/PDF) and transparent PNGs are provided. Ownership transfers upon final payment.

03. What’s the typical timeline?

Usually 2–4 weeks for Essentials and 3–6 weeks for Identity/Brand Kit, depending on scope and feedback speed.

Branding & Logo Design

I design brand identities that are both memorable and functional. Your visuals—logo, color, and typography—are built as a cohesive system, so they scale from social icons to signage and stay consistent across every touchpoint.

  • Discovery & Direction: Brand goals, audience, competitive landscape, and visual positioning.
  • Logo System: Primary mark, secondary/stacked versions, wordmark/submark, and favicon.
  • Color & Typography: Accessible palette with HEX/RGB/CMYK (Pantone on request) and curated font pairings.
  • Usage & Consistency: Clear spacing rules, min sizes, do’s/don’ts, and layout examples.
  • File Deliverables: SVG, EPS, PDF, and transparent PNG exports sized for print and digital.
  • Real-World Readiness: Social profile kit, favicons/app icons, and quick mockups to preview use.
More capabilities
  • Brand Strategy Lite: Positioning notes, tone/voice keywords, and messaging pillars.
  • Creative Direction: Moodboards, references, and rationale behind the visual choices.
  • Collateral Starters: Business cards, letterhead, email signature, and slide cover.
  • Social Media Kit: Canva templates for posts/stories/headers (editable and on-brand).
  • Packaging Extensions: Logo/brand application to boxes, labels, and inserts.
  • Campaign Assets: Launch graphics, ads, banners, and landing hero treatments.
  • Photography & Art Direction: Shot list guidance, styling references, and palette application.
  • Extended Guidelines: 10–20 page brand book with examples and templates.
  • Rebrand/Refresh: Iterative updates to existing logos while preserving equity.
  • Hand-off & Training: Short walkthrough and tips for maintaining consistency.
