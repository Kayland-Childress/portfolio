Frequently Asked Questions

I’ve gathered some of the most common questions clients ask me about my design and development services. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to contact me directly—I’d be happy to chat.

1. What services do you offer?

I specialize in web design, web development, and graphic design. My work includes creating WordPress/Elementor websites, branding and logos, packaging, motion graphics, and digital art. You can explore samples of my work in my portfolio.

2. Do you only work with local clients?

Not at all—I work with clients both locally in Arkansas and remotely worldwide. Thanks to Zoom, email, and shared project tools, we can collaborate seamlessly wherever you are.

3. How much does a website cost?

The cost depends on the project’s size and complexity. A simple portfolio site is usually more affordable, while larger projects with e-commerce or advanced features will be more. I’ll always provide a custom quote after understanding your goals.

4. How long does it take to design a website?

A standard website typically takes 3–6 weeks, depending on revisions, content readiness, and features. Larger or more complex projects may take longer.

5. Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I offer post-launch support and maintenance packages so your website stays secure and up to date. I can also train you on how to update your site if you prefer a hands-on approach.

6. How do I get started?

Simply reach out through my contact form with some details about your project. I’ll schedule a quick call or email consultation to learn more and send you a personalized proposal.
