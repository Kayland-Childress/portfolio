FAQ's

Quick answers to common questions. Click a question to expand.

01. Will I be able to edit the site?

Yes. WordPress + Elementor make editing pages, menus, and posts straightforward. I include a short training handoff.

02. Do you use templates?

I build on flexible frameworks when it benefits speed and maintainability, then customize design, components, and layouts to your brand.

03. Do you handle domains, email, and DNS?

Yes. I can assist with setup/migration, DNS records, and professional email routing (SPF/DKIM/DMARC when applicable).

04. What’s the typical timeline?

Usually 2–4 weeks for Starter, 4–8 weeks for Business, and 6–10 weeks for E-Commerce—once content is ready.

Web Development

I build modern WordPress/Elementor websites that are fast, secure, and easy to manage. Each site blends elegant design with conversion-focused UX so your brand looks polished and performs beautifully.

  • Responsive Design: Polished across desktop, tablet, and mobile with WCAG-minded type, color, and structure.
  • WordPress + Elementor CMS: Reusable sections, clear navigation, and an editor workflow your team can manage without code.
  • Information Architecture & UX: Clear page hierarchy, intuitive navigation, and conversion-focused flows for inquiries, donations, and sales.
  • Performance & SEO Foundations: Core Web Vitals, semantic HTML, clean slugs, metadata/alt text, and image optimization.
  • Forms & Integrations: Contact/donation forms with spam protection, plus CRM, calendar, and email marketing embeds that work seamlessly.
  • Security & Reliability: Updates, backups, hardening best practices, and basic firewall/spam tools to keep things stable.
More capabilities
  • Email Deliverability Setup: SPF/DKIM/DMARC (where applicable) to reduce spam routing.
  • Analytics & Consent: GA4/Meta Pixel, goals/conversions, and cookie/consent banner options.
  • Content Migration & Structure: Organized pages/posts, categories/tags, and purposeful URL strategy.
  • Domain, DNS & Hosting: Setup, transfers, and migrations (e.g., Namecheap ↔ Bluehost).
  • Multilingual & Localization: Language switcher, translated menus, and localized SEO.
  • Donations & Memberships: Payment gateways, recurring gifts, and member-only content.
  • Booking & Events: Calendars, RSVPs, ticketing, and event archives.
  • Reusable Components: Consistent cards, banners, CTAs, and section patterns for faster updates.
  • Policy & Compliance: Privacy/terms pages and accessibility notes.
  • Training & Handoff: Short editor training, simple docs, and roles/permissions.

My process starts with discovery—goals, audience, and site architecture—then moves into wireframes and visual direction aligned to your brand. I develop clean, accessible components in Elementor, structure content for clarity, and optimize for search with semantic HTML, purposeful URLs, and on-page metadata.

Before launch, I complete quality checks, performance tuning, and email deliverability basics (SPF/DKIM/DMARC when applicable). I can assist with domain/DNS and host migration, and I offer optional maintenance for updates, backups, and security—so your site stays fast, stable, and easy to edit long after launch.
